  How it works  

PETFinder WORKS IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS.

1 - SEARCH:

It's simple. On PETFinder hompage enter the name of a shelter you are looking for and click on search. You can also click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all shelters

2 - LOCATE:

Get to see where the shelter is located by clicking on the shelter profile. It's even more exciting. You can get directions from anywhere in the country.

3 - Contact:

Contact the located shelter for more information. Click on the CALL ME button and you will be contacted back right away. We will also make sure you will be contacted on time.

4 - RELAX

Once you will be contacted, you can relax. We will take care of you concerns, whether is about your pet or if you want to adopt one.
